# Shakespeareanify - Chrome Extension

This extension converts thy modern words into fair Shakespearean text as thou typest!

## 🛠️ Installation Instructions:
1. **Download & Extract**  
   - Download the folder containing this extension.  
   - Ensure all files are in the same directory.

2. **Load the Extension in Chrome**
   - Open Google Chrome.
   - Go to `chrome://extensions/` in the address bar.
   - Enable "Developer mode" (toggle in the top-right corner).
   - Click "Load unpacked" and select the folder where you extracted the files.

3. **Using the Extension**
   - Click on the extension icon in the toolbar.
   - Press "Enable" to activate Shakespearean mode.
   - Type in any text field, and your words shall be transformed into Shakespearean style.

4. **Disabling the Extension**
   - Click on the extension icon again.
   - Press "Disable" to return to modern English.

5. **Uninstalling**
   - Go to `chrome://extensions/`, find "Shakespeareanify," and click "Remove."

## 🎭 Features:
✅ Translates words to Shakespearean as you type.  
✅ Works on any website with input fields.  
✅ Easily toggle the feature on or off.  

Enjoy speaking like the Bard! 🎭
